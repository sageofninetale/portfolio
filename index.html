About Me

I don't just analyse data. I obsess over the story it's hiding. With 3 years of experience across fintech, housing, consulting, and IT services, I've built a career on turning messy, complex information into clarity that actually drives decisions.

I thrive where the answers aren't obvious. The harder the problem, the more curious I get. I've validated multi-source datasets, mapped broken processes, rebuilt KPI frameworks from scratch, and walked into rooms full of senior stakeholders to say "here's what the numbers are actually telling us." Each challenge has made me sharper, more resilient, and honestly more excited about the next one.

Beyond the day job, I build things because I genuinely love it. I'm the founder of Cascade AI and Savora, products I designed, built, and shipped independently. I prototype AI solutions, experiment with machine learning models, and chase that satisfying moment when something I built actually helps someone. Not because I have to. Because I can't help it.

Football has shaped everything I am. I've been playing for over 10 years and it taught me discipline, resilience, and a never-give-up attitude before I even knew what those words meant professionally. I carry that same mentality into everything I do, on the pitch and off it. Arsenal is the club, always has been, always will be, even when the season tests every bit of that resilience.

I also play tennis, grew up playing cricket, and get to the gym five days a week, not just for the physical side but because it keeps my mind sharp and focused. When it comes to food, growing up my mum handled everything. Once I moved to the UK I had to figure it out myself and turns out I was pretty good at it. My friends say the food is absolutely banging and honestly I have to agree. Cooking calms my mind in a way nothing else quite does. And whenever I get the chance I travel, not the planned, itinerary-heavy kind. I prefer getting lost in a city and figuring it out as I go. That's where the best stories come from.

I show up fully for everything I do. Whether that's a dashboard, a side project, a meal, a match, or a new city. If that sounds like the kind of person you want on your team, let's talk.
